Our certificate & online programs for 2025

Shaping Future Educators: Courses Offered at Garissa Teachers Training College in 2025

1. Diploma in Primary Teacher Education (DPTE): Building Strong Foundations

Teaching at the primary level requires more than just knowledge—it demands patience, creativity, and a deep understanding of how young minds learn. Our Diploma in Primary Teacher Education (DPTE) is designed to equip future teachers with the necessary skills to shape young learners effectively. The program covers child psychology, pedagogy, and modern teaching methodologies, ensuring graduates are well-prepared to nurture the next generation. With a hands-on approach, students engage in real classroom experiences, making them confident and competent educators.


2. Diploma in Secondary Teacher Education (DSTE): Inspiring the Leaders of Tomorrow

Secondary school teachers play a crucial role in shaping the future of students by guiding them through critical academic years. Our Diploma in Secondary Teacher Education (DSTE) program focuses on subject specialization, innovative teaching techniques, and classroom management skills. With access to well-equipped facilities and expert faculty, students gain in-depth knowledge of their chosen subjects while developing the ability to mentor and motivate learners. This program is perfect for those who aspire to make a meaningful impact in secondary education.!


3. Certificate in Early Childhood Development Education (ECDE): Nurturing Young Minds

The early years of a child’s education lay the foundation for lifelong learning, making early childhood education a vital profession. Our Certificate in Early Childhood Development Education (ECDE) program is tailored for individuals passionate about working with young children. The course focuses on child development, creative teaching strategies, and classroom engagement techniques. Graduates of this program become skilled caregivers and educators, ready to create a nurturing and stimulating learning environment for preschoolers.


4. Professional Development Courses: Enhancing Teaching Skills

Continuous learning is essential for educators to stay updated with modern teaching trends. Our Professional Development Courses are short, specialized programs designed to help teachers enhance their skills in areas such as digital learning, special needs education, and school leadership. These courses cater to both aspiring and experienced teachers who want to refine their expertise and keep up with evolving educational practices. By enrolling in these courses, teachers can elevate their careers and bring innovative teaching approaches into their classrooms.
