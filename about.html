About Us

We are North eastern Biggest university

Garissa Teachers Training College is a premier institution dedicated to shaping the future of education in Kenya. With a strong commitment to academic excellence and professional development, the college provides high-quality teacher training programs designed to equip educators with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to inspire and transform young minds. Our institution offers a diverse range of diploma and certificate courses tailored to meet the dynamic needs of the education sector. Through a blend of theory and practical training, we prepare our students to become competent, innovative, and passionate educators capable of making a lasting impact in their communities. Beyond academics, Garissa Teachers Training College fosters a vibrant learning environment enriched with state-of-the-art facilities, a well-equipped library, and modern technology to support student success. Our dedicated faculty and supportive community ensure that every trainee receives personalized guidance and mentorship throughout their journey. At Garissa Teachers Training College, we believe in empowering educators to drive positive change. Whether you are starting your teaching career or seeking to enhance your skills, our institution provides the perfect foundation for excellence in education. Join us in shaping the future, one teacher at a time!
