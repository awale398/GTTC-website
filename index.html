Courses we Offer

Garissa Teachers Training College offers diverse programs to equip aspiring educators with essential skills. Our courses include a Diploma in Primary Teacher Education (DPTE) for future primary school teachers, a Certificate in Early Childhood Development Education (ECDE) for nurturing young learners, and a Diploma in Secondary Teacher Education (DSTE) for secondary school teaching. We also provide short professional development courses to enhance teaching skills and education management. Join us and be part of the future of education!.

DPTE

Diploma in Primary Teacher Education - A comprehensive program for those seeking to become professional primary school teachers.

ECDE

Certificate in Early Childhood Development Education - Focused on foundational childhood education, nurturing young learners.

DSTE

Diploma in Secondary Teacher Education - Preparing future secondary school teachers with subject specialization.

Our Garrisa Campus

Garissa Teachers Training College boasts well-equipped campuses in Garissa, providing a serene and conducive environment for learning. Our campuses feature modern classrooms, a well-stocked library, advanced ICT facilities, and spacious recreational areas to support both academic and personal growth. Surrounded by a vibrant community, the college offers a peaceful atmosphere where students can focus on their studies while engaging in extracurricular activities. With a commitment to excellence, our campuses serve as a hub for aspiring educators to build their careers and make a lasting impact in the education sector.

Our Facilities

Garissa Teachers Training College offers modern facilities, including well-equipped classrooms, a resourceful library, advanced ICT labs, and spacious recreational areas. Our serene learning environment ensures students have the best resources to excel academically and professionally.

World Class Library

The library at Garissa Teachers Training College is a haven for learning, offering a rich collection of books, research materials, and digital resources to support academic excellence. With a quiet and comfortable study environment, students have access to the knowledge they need to grow, explore, and excel in their teaching careers.

Largest PlayGround

The playground at Garissa Teachers Training College provides a vibrant space for students to unwind, stay active, and engage in sports. Designed to promote physical well-being and teamwork, it fosters a healthy balance between academics and recreation in a lively and welcoming environment.

Tasty & Healthy Food

The cafeteria at Garissa Teachers Training College offers a warm and inviting space where students can enjoy nutritious meals in a relaxed setting. With a variety of delicious and affordable options, it serves as a perfect place to refresh, socialize, and recharge for the day’s activities.

About Us
