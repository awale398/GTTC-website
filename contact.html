About Us

Garissa Teachers Training College is a center of excellence dedicated to shaping the next generation of educators. With a strong commitment to quality training, we provide a nurturing environment where passion for teaching meets innovation. Our well-equipped campuses, modern facilities, and experienced faculty create the perfect foundation for students to develop their skills and thrive in the education sector. Beyond academics, we foster a culture of growth, leadership, and community engagement, ensuring our graduates are not just teachers but mentors and role models. At Garissa TTC, we believe in empowering educators to inspire, transform, and make a lasting impact on society.
